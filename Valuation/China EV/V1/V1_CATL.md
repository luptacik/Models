# CATL R1 Company Research Dossier

**Contemporary Amperex Technology Co., Limited (宁德时代)**
**300750.SZ (Shenzhen A-share) | 3750.HK (Hong Kong H-share)**
**Reporting Currency: CNY (RMB) | Fiscal Year End: December 31**
**Date Compiled: April 4, 2026**

---

CATL is the world's dominant battery manufacturer, commanding **39.2% global EV battery market share** — its ninth consecutive year at #1 and 2.4× the #2 player (BYD at 16.4%). FY2025 delivered revenue of **RMB 423.7B (+17% YoY)** and net profit of **RMB 72.2B (+42% YoY)**, with operating cash flow surging to **RMB 133.2B**. The company operates at **96.9% capacity utilization** across 772 GWh of installed capacity, with 321 GWh under construction. Its HK listing in May 2025 raised HK$41B, and the company has committed to a **50% dividend payout ratio** for three consecutive years.
