## KEY ASSUMPTIONS & RISKS

### Why CATL could be worth MORE than the base case:
1. **ESS acceleration:** If energy storage grows to 25%+ of revenue by 2028 at higher margins, the blended profitability improves significantly
2. **International mix shift:** Overseas gross margin is 31.4% vs domestic 24.0% — every 5pp increase in overseas mix adds ~37bps to company-level margin
3. **Na-ion disruption:** If sodium-ion scales to 50+ GWh by 2028, it opens new price-sensitive segments that CATL's competitors cannot access
4. **Choco-Swap platform value:** At 30,000+ stations, the recurring service revenue stream could be worth RMB 50-100B+

### Why CATL could be worth LESS:
1. **BYD insourcing:** BYD is the world's #2 battery maker and its own largest customer — every BYD unit sold is a CATL unit not sold
2. **Geopolitical escalation:** US DOD designation + FEOC rules could spread to EU/allied markets, restricting CATL's international expansion
3. **Lithium price volatility:** A sustained recovery above RMB 150,000/ton could compress margins if CATL cannot pass through
4. **Solid-state disruption:** Toyota/Samsung mass production by 2028-2030 could challenge CATL's technology leadership
5. **A/H premium compression:** If the H-share premium to A-shares narrows from current ~44% toward the historical ~20%, H-share price would decline even if fundamentals hold
