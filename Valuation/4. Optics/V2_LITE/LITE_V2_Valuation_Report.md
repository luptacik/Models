**Lumentum is the most competitively advantaged company in the optics sector — and also the most overvalued.** At $827, the stock trades at 16.8x NTM revenue and ~59x FY2027 consensus earnings, commanding a 121% raw multiple premium to the peer group median. Five of six valuation methods indicate 24-57% downside from current levels. The weighted base case price target is **$466**, representing **44% downside**.

The bull case for LITE is extraordinarily compelling: NVIDIA Vera Rubin lock-in via $2B convertible preferred, EML laser monopoly with supply sold out through 2027, OCS backlog >$400M, and a credible path to $2B/quarter revenue at 40% operating margins. The moat scored 5/5 in the sector analysis — the highest in the peer group. But the market has already priced this in and more. At current levels, every optimistic assumption must break perfectly to avoid material losses.

**Conviction: 3/5** — world-class moat, but extreme valuation caps conviction.
