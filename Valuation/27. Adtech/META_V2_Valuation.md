2. EU DMA "Less Personalized Ads" causes material European revenue impact (management flagged as risk)
3. US youth-related litigation produces material judgment (management flagged as possible)
4. AI search disruption (ChatGPT, Perplexity) broadens to social discovery
5. RL losses accelerate above guided $19B run-rate

**Upside risks** (could push base case toward bull):
1. Advantage+ fully automated "goal-only" ad system hits end-2026 target, driving another leg of pricing power
2. RL losses finally plateau as guided, allowing market to stop discounting the segment
3. Capex intensity moderates post-2026 as AI infrastructure build-out matures
4. Additional buyback authorization (current authorization remaining not disclosed in V1)
5. Cash tax benefits from OBBBA begin flowing through in 2026, amplifying net income growth
