- **eCommerce expansion is binary** — if AXON doesn't translate to eCom ROAS at scale, TAM thesis collapses
- **Meta Advantage+ ($60B run-rate)** directly competing for same performance budgets
- **Apple/Google platform policy** — ATT, fingerprinting restrictions, Chrome Privacy Sandbox killed (Feb 2026) removes one overhang but eliminates urgency for alt-IDs
- **Take rate sustainability** — FY25 growth was ~100% yield (revenue per install +72%, installs +3%); further yield compounding requires continuous AXON improvement

### Macro / Cyclical
- Performance-based mobile gaming UA is discretionary ad spend — recession sensitivity material
- 90% of ad buyers expressing tariff/macro concern (sector handoff)
