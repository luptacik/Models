# Palo Alto Networks (PANW) valuation-grade research dossier

**Palo Alto Networks is the world's largest pure-play cybersecurity company, executing a $28B+ M&A spree to build a four-pillar security platform while delivering 30%+ non-GAAP operating margins and ~37% FCF margins.** The stock trades at ~$147, down ~20% YTD, pressured by CyberArk integration costs that lowered EPS guidance and by the Anthropic Mythos AI disruption scare. CEO Nikesh Arora's $10M open-market stock purchase on March 27, 2026 — his first in over five years — signals strong insider conviction at current levels. With $6.33B in NGS ARR growing 33%, $16B in RPO, and a zero-debt balance sheet (pre-CyberArk), PANW remains the dominant cybersecurity platform but faces a critical integration execution test over the next four quarters.
