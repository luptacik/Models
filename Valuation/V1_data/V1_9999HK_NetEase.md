## MODULE 9: COMPETITIVE POSITIONING SNAPSHOT

**1. Market Share**
NetEase is China's #2 gaming company by revenue. Tencent + NetEase hold ~60%+ combined share of the Chinese gaming market. ✓ (R1 sector analysis) NetEase's online games revenue of RMB 89.6B represents approximately 18–20% of China's domestic gaming market (~RMB 350–400B+). ~

**2. Key Differentiator**
In-house R&D scale and self-developed IP. NetEase operates one of the largest in-house game R&D teams globally, with R&D spending at RMB 17.7B (15.7% of revenue). ✓ This enables 20+ year franchise longevity (Fantasy Westward Journey), AI-native gameplay features, and cross-platform/global launches (Marvel Rivals). The depth of self-developed IP means high gross margins (70.5% in games Q4 2025) with minimal royalty leakage. ✓

**3. Biggest Competitive Threat**
Tencent Holdings (0700.HK) — controls WeChat distribution (1.3B+ MAU), owns Riot Games, Supercell, and has international gaming revenue of RMB 21.1B in Q4 2025 alone (+32% YoY). Tencent's AI investment is doubling to ~RMB 36B+ in 2026, providing scale advantages in AI-powered game development. ✓ (R3 sector analysis)

Secondary threat: miHoYo/HoYoverse (private) — Genshin Impact / Honkai: Star Rail demonstrate that smaller studios can capture outsized global share with a single breakout franchise.

**4. Customer Switching Costs: MEDIUM**
Gaming has inherently lower switching costs than enterprise software. However, NetEase's long-running franchises (Fantasy Westward Journey, Identity V) create substantial invested time, social communities, and in-game asset accumulation that raise effective switching costs for core player bases. New title success depends on gameplay quality, not lock-in.

**5. Pricing Power Evidence**
- Games & VAS gross margin expanded from 66.7% to 70.5% (Q4 2024 → Q4 2025) ✓ — driven by favorable product mix (more self-developed, less licensed)
- Deferred revenue surged +34.1% YoY to RMB 20.5B ✓ — indicates strong advance monetization
- Apple China commission cut (30% → 25%) provides structural margin tailwind starting March 2026 ✓
