## MODULE 5: MANAGEMENT GUIDANCE & LONG-TERM TARGETS

### 5A. Current guidance

⚠️ **Trip.com historically does NOT provide specific numeric quarterly or annual guidance.** CFO Cindy Wang stated: "We actually never set a specific margin target. Instead, we consider margin as a natural outcome of a dynamic business mix and continuous improvement in our operational efficiency." (Q4 2024 earnings call) ✓

**Qualitative forward commentary from Q4 2025 earnings call (Feb 25, 2026):**

- **Inbound travel target:** Aim to double inbound traveler numbers to ~40M in 2026 (from ~20M in 2025)
- **International expansion:** Continued heavy investment in APAC markets, product localization
- **AI investment:** Ongoing TripGenie / DeepTrip AI infrastructure investment
- **Capital return:** $5B share repurchase authorization; annual plan includes buyback + ~$200M dividend
- **No specific FY2026 revenue, margin, or EPS guidance provided** ✓

- **International TAM:** Asian markets outside China represent "150% of the China market TAM" (Q4 2024 call)
- **No formal analyst day / capital markets day identified** in 2025 or early 2026
