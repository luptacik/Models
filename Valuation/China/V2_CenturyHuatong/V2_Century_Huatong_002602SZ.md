**Purpose:** What growth rate is the market pricing in?

**Assumptions:** Current EV ¥117.2B; base year revenue ¥38,000M (FY2025E); current FCF margin 20.8%; WACC 12% (premium for A-share governance risk, hit concentration, regulatory tail risk, FX risk — standard China internet is 10–11%, Century Huatong +1–2% for fraud history and share pledge); terminal FCF margin 25%; terminal growth 3%; 10-year projection.

**Result:** The market implies a **~4.8% revenue CAGR** over 10 years.

This is materially below the consensus 2-year CAGR of **15.3%** (FY2025→FY2027) and well below the current actual growth rate of +68%. The market is pricing in rapid deceleration — essentially assuming Whiteout Survival matures quickly and no new blockbuster emerges.

**Interpretation:** This is REASONABLE as a risk-adjusted view given the hit-driven model. However, if FY2026 consensus (+22%) materializes, the stock is undervalued at ¥17.04. The implied growth gap is the primary mispricing signal.
