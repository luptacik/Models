## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Trip.com is **meaningfully undervalued**. The reverse DCF reveals the market is pricing in a **−2.9% revenue CAGR** over the next decade — implying the company's revenue will *shrink* — versus 17% actual growth and 16% consensus for FY2026. This 20 percentage-point gap between implied and actual growth is the most extreme mispricing signal in the current Chinese internet coverage universe.

All six valuation methods produce prices above HK$390.6. The methods cluster into two groups: the near-term anchored methods (Comps at HK$496, SoP at HK$429, LBO Floor at HK$432) and the fundamental/forward methods (DCF base at HK$772, M&A at HK$510). The tension reflects the structural "China discount" — a geopolitical risk premium embedded in all Chinese ADR/HK-listed stocks — which compresses observable multiples below what pure DCF would suggest.

The triangulated base case of **HK$599** represents a pragmatic middle ground: the China discount exists and should be respected, but at ~2.5x EV/NTM revenue for a company growing 17% with 81% gross margins and 30% FCF margins, it has gone too far. The 38-analyst consensus price target of US$78.75 (≈HK$613) corroborates this view.

**Key risk:** The SAMR anti-monopoly investigation (active since January 2026) and associated US securities class actions create genuine binary risk. A worst-case 10% fine would cost ~RMB 6.25B (~$890M), or roughly one year of estimated FCF. Alibaba's stock *rose* 7-9% on its penalty day due to uncertainty resolution — Trip.com may follow a similar pattern.

## METHOD 4: M&A COMPS

Recent pure-play OTA M&A is extremely limited — the global OTA sector is a 3-player oligopoly (Booking, Expedia, Trip.com), making comparable transactions scarce.

**Historical OTA transactions:**
- Skyscanner (2016, Trip.com acquired): ~14x revenue at ~40% growth
- MakeMyTrip (2019, Trip.com invested): ~7x revenue at ~30% growth
- Farelogix (2023, Accelya acquired): ~6x revenue at ~10% growth

**Takeout valuation:**
- Applicable multiple: 4.5-7.0x revenue → Implied EV range: RMB 281-437B
- After 25% control premium discount: RMB 211-328B
- **Mid-point implied price: HK$510**

A full takeout is LOW probability at $35B market cap. The $5B buyback acts as a synthetic floor.
