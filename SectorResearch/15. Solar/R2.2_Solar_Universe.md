# SECTOR-LEVEL OBSERVATIONS

## Key cycle dynamics confirmed by the data

1. **Polysilicon/wafer downcycle is brutal and ongoing.** Tongwei H1 2025 GM -4.7% in solar segment; GCL Tech H1 GM -12.2%; Daqo H1 GM -34%. Even the largest, lowest-cost producers (Tongwei) cannot earn money at current poly prices. Late-June 2025 saw a 36% price rebound on anti-involution policy intervention — first sign of cycle bottom but unconfirmed.

2. **Inverter / ESS bifurcation is the clearest theme.** Sungrow's ESS revenue doubled in H1 2025 (+128% YoY); reached 41.8% of FY2025 revenue. CATL ESS supply-constrained at 36 GWh per quarter. FLNC backlog record. ESS demand is real, accelerating, and policy-supported. Pure-play module exposure is the worst place to be; integrated inverter+ESS is the best.

3. **US tariff wall + IRA = structural advantage for US-domiciled or US-capacity-bearing names.** Shoals tariffs cost ~80bps GM but the moat keeps Chinese competition at bay. Hanwha's entire FY2026 thesis is the Cartersville ramp. Waaree's US capacity (2.6 GW) commands premium pricing.

4. **Indian solar is the structural growth story China cannot disrupt.** Waaree +70% revenue growth, Tata Power renewables +78%. PLI scheme + tariff wall + domestic demand growth = no competition.

5. **Data center grid demand is the cleanest signal in the dataset.** ETN Electrical Americas backlog +31%; data center orders +200%. This is the pickaxe play for AI infrastructure spend.

## Three sharpest fundamental-vs-price divergences (catalyst/trap calls)

🎯 **Sungrow (300274.SZ) — POSITIVE DIVERGENCE.** Combines the only positive elements in Chinese solar (ESS scale, overseas mix, margin expansion, dual H-share listing optionality) without polysilicon downcycle exposure. FY2025 net profit +22% in a sector where most peers are loss-making. The cleanest long in the universe.

🎯 **Waaree Energies (WAAREEENER.NS) — POSITIVE DIVERGENCE.** Single best growth profile (+70% revenue) with structural protection from Chinese price collapse. Order book of 24 GW gives multi-year visibility. The only clean way to play global solar demand growth.

⚠️ **Samsung SDI (006400.KS) — NEGATIVE DIVERGENCE / VALUE TRAP.** -20% revenue, -13% operating margin, Stellantis JV exit risk, weakest IRA position of the three Korean cells, Cartersville-style execution risk on the StarPlus retool. Tesla deal optionality is real but small relative to the structural problem.
