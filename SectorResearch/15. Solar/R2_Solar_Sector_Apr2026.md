## ⚠️ CRITICAL CONTEXT — READ FIRST

**Section 25D Residential Clean Energy Credit EXPIRED December 31, 2025.**

This is a sector-defining policy event embedded in the **One Big Beautiful Bill Act (OBBBA / H.R. 1, P.L. 119-21)**, signed by President Trump July 4, 2025. The 30% homeowner-collected ITC is **dead** as of January 1, 2026 — no phase-down, no extension, IRS-issued FAQs confirm strict interpretation. This single legislative event reorders the relative positioning of every name in the universe.

| Credit | Status (April 2026) | Affected Names |
|---|---|---|
| **§25D** Residential ITC (homeowner-owned) | **TERMINATED** Dec 31, 2025 | ENPH (devastating), SEDG (devastating), GNRC PWRcell (negative), homeowner-owned channels broadly |
| **§48E** Commercial / TPO ITC | **Preserved** — must start construction by July 4, 2026 OR be placed in service by Dec 31, 2027 | RUN (massive structural advantage — only TPO model retains credit access through lease/PPA structures) |
| **§45X** Advanced Manufacturing Production Credit | **Preserved** through current FSLR 2026 guidance | FSLR (~$2.1B 2026 expected), ENPH (sold $235M for $214M in March 2026 — 91¢/$1) |
| **§45Y/48E utility-scale** | **Strict enforcement per Trump executive order July 7** — "begin construction" definition tightened (likely substantial physical work, not deposits) | FSLR utility customers, all utility-scale developers |
| Domestic Content Bonus (10%) | Preserved with FEOC sourcing thresholds: 40% in 2026, rising to 60% by 2030 | Penalty for any name using Chinese components |
