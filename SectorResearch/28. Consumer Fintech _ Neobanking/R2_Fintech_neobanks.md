## NOTES & METHODOLOGY

1. **Recency:** All data from most recent reported quarter (predominantly Q4 2025 / Dec 2025). Exceptions: AFRM (FQ2'26 = Dec 2025), CHYM (Q3'25 latest official), RELY (Q3'25 latest official).

2. **Revenue vs. Gross Profit:** For SQ/Block, gross profit is the primary operating metric; total revenue includes bitcoin and Cash App P2P pass-through. For FOUR/Shift4, GRLNF (Gross Revenue Less Network Fees) is the primary metric.

3. **International Companies:** MELI, SE, GRAB, KLAR report in USD. NU reports in USD (IFRS). WISE, DLO, ZIP report in local currencies — not yet converted.

4. **Insurance Companies (OSCR, LMND, ROOT):** Standard fintech metrics (ARPU, NCO) don't directly apply. Insurance-specific metrics (IFP, loss ratio, combined ratio) used where available.

5. **Acquired/Delisted:** ML (MoneyLion) acquired by Gen Digital — no standalone data. MQ (Marqeta) status requires verification.

6. **Valuation Multiples:** Approximated from market data as of late March 2026. Precise NTM consensus requires terminal data (Bloomberg/FactSet/Visible Alpha).
