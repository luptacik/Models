# Nuclear Renaissance Sector: Standardized Metrics Table

**Uranium spot price reference: ~$92/lb U3O8 (early April 2026) | Long-term contract price: ~$90/lb**

This report covers 13 entities across six nuclear sub-sectors using the most recent official filings (Q4 2025 / FY2025) and market data as of early April 2026. **Kazatomprom leads on profitability (62.9% EBITDA margin) and lowest leverage (0.18x)**, while Constellation Energy dominates on scale (~22 GW nuclear fleet, $25.5B revenue). Among growth-stage names, BWXT's **50% backlog growth** and 1.68x book-to-bill stand out as the strongest demand signal. Pre-revenue SMR developers Oklo and NuScale collectively hold ~$3.5B in cash but carry meaningful execution risk. The sector trades at premium multiples—median EV/EBITDA of ~35x for profitable companies—reflecting the market's pricing of secular nuclear demand growth from AI data centers and energy security.
