## Key sector-level observations

**AppLovin's dominance is extraordinary.** With **87.9% gross margins, 75.8% GAAP operating margins, and 72.1% FCF margins**, APP operates at profitability levels unprecedented in adtech — or arguably in all of software. Its $5.5B TTM revenue grew 66% YoY, creating a $141B market cap that exceeds the next four pure-play adtech companies combined. The risk: an SEC investigation and a **17.8x EV/NTM revenue** multiple that prices in sustained hypergrowth.

**The valuation dispersion is extreme.** CRTO trades at **0.30x EV/NTM revenue** — the cheapest in the group — while APP trades at 17.8x. Among pure-plays, TBLA (0.42x), CRTO (0.30x), APPS (1.1x), and DSP (1.15x) all trade below 1.5x revenue. The market is aggressively bifurcating between AI-powered growth stories (APP, RDDT) and legacy/transitioning models.

**Reddit is the breakout story in walled gardens.** At **+70% revenue growth, 91.2% gross margins, and 31.0% FCF margins**, RDDT posted the fastest growth and highest gross margin of any company in this universe. Its **+75% advertiser growth and +42% ARPU expansion** suggest monetization is still early-innings. The $23.6B EV/~7x NTM revenue prices this in, but if growth sustains above 40%, multiples have room.

**CTV is the dominant growth vector.** MGNI's CTV contribution crossed majority status in Q1 2026. DSP's CTV share hit 46% of ad spend. ROKU's platform revenue (essentially all CTV) grew 18%. TTD attributes ~50% of its business to video/CTV. This confirms CTV as the structural winner in programmatic, and companies without CTV exposure (APPS, TBLA) are at a strategic disadvantage.

**SBC dilution is the sector's hidden tax.** Three walled garden names — **PINS (20.8%), RDDT (17.6%), SNAP (17.1%)** — deliver over 17% of revenue in stock-based compensation. Among pure-plays, **TTD (16.9%)** and **ZETA (13.6%)** stand out. In contrast, CRTO (3.8%), APP (~3-4%), and WPP (0.5%) are notably disciplined. For any FCF-based valuation, SBC adjustment is essential.

**WPP's decline stands alone.** At **-5.4% organic revenue decline** with Q4 accelerating to -6.9%, WPP is the only company in this universe experiencing meaningful structural revenue contraction. Its Elevate28 restructuring (£500M cost savings, dividend cut 62%, goodwill write-downs) signals a multi-year turnaround. By contrast, Publicis delivered its 7th consecutive year of outperformance at +5.6% organic growth with 18.2% operating margins.

**AI is restructuring the entire sector.** Baidu's AI-native marketing services grew **+301% to RMB 9.8B**. NAVER attributes 55% of its ad growth to AI. META's Advantage+ AI tools hit a **$10B run rate**. APP's AXON 2.0 AI engine powers its extraordinary margins. Reddit Max AI campaigns deliver 17% lower CPA. The companies that have embedded AI deepest into their ad stacks are growing fastest and earning the highest margins — the correlation is now unmistakable.
